Our Story

About Prompt Doctor

Born from a passion for AI and a frustration with poor prompts — Prompt Doctor is the platform that turns anyone into a prompt engineering expert.

Our Mission

Making AI work better
for everyone

Prompt Doctor was built on a simple belief: the quality of your AI output is only as good as the quality of your input. Most people leave enormous value on the table simply because they don't know how to communicate effectively with AI.

We set out to change that. By combining intelligent analysis, real-time feedback, and a rich learning ecosystem, Prompt Doctor empowers students, developers, marketers, and creators to unlock the true potential of AI tools.

From a college project at the College of Engineering Phaltan to a full-featured platform — our journey is driven by curiosity, collaboration, and a commitment to making AI accessible to all.

Platform Features

Everything Prompt Doctor offers

A complete ecosystem for mastering prompt engineering — from analysis to education.

AI Prompt Analyzer

The core of Prompt Doctor. Paste any prompt and receive an instant, detailed analysis covering overall score (out of 10), clarity rating, creativity rating, and structure feedback. The engine detects vague language, missing context, weak action verbs, and structural gaps — then delivers both an improved professional version and a creatively enhanced version of your prompt.

Score /10ClarityCreativity StructureIssue DetectionAI Rewrite

Prompt Template Library

A curated library of 17+ professionally crafted prompt templates spanning 5 categories. Each template is designed by prompt engineering experts and can be copied to clipboard or sent directly to the analyzer with one click. Categories include Content Writing, Coding, Marketing, Image Generation, and Business Ideas.

Content WritingCodingMarketing Image GenerationBusiness IdeasOne-Click Copy

Prompt Engineering Academy

A comprehensive learning hub covering everything from beginner fundamentals to advanced techniques. Includes 12 structured tip cards, good vs. bad prompt comparisons with real examples, and a 60-question interactive quiz with instant feedback, score tracking, and a results screen. Topics span Chain-of-Thought, RAG, ReAct, Tree of Thoughts, and more.

Beginner TipsAdvanced TechniquesGood vs Bad 60 Quiz QuestionsScore Tracking

Personal Dashboard

A full analytics dashboard that tracks your prompt engineering journey. View your complete prompt history with scores, visualize your score trend over time with a canvas-rendered line chart, see your average score, personal best, and weekly activity. Prompts can be copied or reused directly from the history list. All data persists via localStorage.

Score Trend ChartPrompt HistoryAvg Score Personal BestLocal Storage

Aria — AI Chat Assistant

A floating AI chatbot available on every page. Aria is your personal prompt engineering coach — she answers questions about clarity, structure, creativity, coding prompts, and general best practices. With context-aware responses and a friendly conversational UI, Aria makes learning feel natural and accessible at any moment.

Always AvailableContext-AwarePrompt Tips Conversational UIAll Pages

Premium Design System

Built with a luxury gold-and-black aesthetic featuring glassmorphism cards, 3D hover effects, smooth CSS animations, a responsive grid layout, and a custom canvas chart. The design is fully responsive across mobile and desktop, with a loading overlay, scroll-reveal animations, toast notifications, and a consistent design language across all 5 pages.
